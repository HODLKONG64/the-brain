## IMAGE GENERATION & ON-BRAND CONSISTENCY RULES

### Core Principle
Images must represent characters faithfully according to the established definitions, ensuring visual consistency across all outputs.

### Primary Reference Sources
Use the following reference sources for image generation:
- Official concept art
- Character designs from approved games or media
- Style guides that define character aesthetics

### If NO Reference Images Can Be Found (fallback rules)
- Use speculative design based on character traits and lore.
- Prioritize visual elements that are most prominent in the character's description.

### How the Agent Ties Images to the Final Lore Details
Images must be cross-referenced with the character's final lore details to maintain narrative coherence. This includes:
- Aligning appearance with character backstories.
- Ensuring visual cues correspond with character development arcs.

### Enforcement & Consistency Checks
Regular audits must be performed to ensure adherence to these guidelines:
- Peer reviews for image submissions.
- Feedback loops with character designers and writers to address inconsistencies.

### Why These Rules
These rules ensure that the brand's imagery remains consistent, enhancing audience recognition and engagement with the characters and their stories.

## BLOCK TOPIA UNIVERSE — COMPLETE CHARACTER REGISTRY

### Tier 1: Primary Protagonists
- **Alfie "Bitcoin KiD" Blaze** — Protagonist; fisherman, DJ, graffiti artist, Web3 builder. UK urban aesthetic, stocky build, always slightly paint-stained. Signature yellow bonnet. Anchor of the entire Block Topia universe.
- **Jodie Zoom 2000** — Visionary tech entrepreneur and futurist mentor. First to articulate the economic theory of Block Topia. Appears at Windermere and key strategy sessions.
- **Sarah "Queen P-fly"** — Strategic mind; NFT market oracle; reads market cycles before they break. Power broker aligned with the Crowned Royal Moongirls.
- **Lady-INK** — Mysterious muse; train painter; guide between epochs. Ageless, ethereal, train-themed imagery. Origins unknown. Never fully explained — mystery maintained at all times.

### Tier 2: Secondary Pillars
- **Elder Codex-7** — Ancient wisdom keeper; blockchain philosopher. Real name unknown. Face never shown. Distributed the first pamphlets in 2009 Shoreditch. Source of the Eternal Codex.
- **NULL The Prophet** — Void theory philosopher; chaos and order balance advocate. Major antagonist / philosophical counterweight. Predicted the Final Fork.
- **HODL Warrior Commander** — Diamond-hand leader; resistance fighter. Commands the HODL Warriors faction. Appears at fishing lakes as an unexpected encounter.

### Tier 3: Faction Leaders & Collective Characters
- **Crowned Royal Moongirls** — Female founders and power brokers. Multiple members. Appear as a council. Female leadership layer of Block Topia.
- **Bald-Headed Moonboys Set 1** — Foot soldiers; street-level evangelists. First wave. Expressive, varied personalities.
- **Bald-Headed Moonboys Set 2** — Second wave; bonnet variations. Slightly different energy from Set 1. Both sets rotate in dream sequences.
- **Bitcoin X Kids (3 paths)** — Youth splinter movements; tech rebels. Three distinct factions formed from a single origin point.
- **OG Bitcoin Kids** — Original believers; elder street philosophers. Pre-date the Moonboys. Living proof of the 2009 epoch.
- **GraffPUNKS Network Agents** — Art collective members; blockchain revolutionaries. Manage the GraffPUNKS Network Radio. Core to the Art Insurgency pillar.

### The 6 Pillars (Faction Structure)
1. **Pillar of Creation** — GraffPUNKS, Art Insurgency, Dream Weavers
2. **Pillar of Economics** — DeFi Dragons, HODL Warriors, Market Makers
