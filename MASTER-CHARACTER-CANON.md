## 🌍 UNIVERSAL TIMELINE & SETTING

### **The Crypto Moonboys Universe: Multiverse Overview**

The Crypto Moonboys universe is a **decentralized, lore-driven metaverse** spanning from present-day London (1980s–2026) through a dystopian blockchain-based future (Year 3008). The narrative explores themes of:
- **Cultural Preservation** via blockchain permanence ("unbuffable" art)
- **Reality Fragmentation** after the Triple Fork Event (2198)
- **Faction Warfare** across 40 factions vying for dominance
- **Forkborn Seers** (like Jodie Zoom) reshaping timelines with Echo Ink
- **Philosophical War:** Deterministic Order (Block Topia) vs. Anarchic Freedom (Street Kingdoms)

### **Historical Epochs**

#### **EPOCH 1: The Golden Age of Graffiti (1980s–2012)**
- **Setting:** Real-world London streets
- **Key Events:** 
  - Graffiti Kings (GK) collective founded by Darren Cullen (SER)
  - Street art rebellion against establishment erasure
  - 2012 London Olympics sponsorship (legitimacy through art)
- **Philosophy:** Graffiti as first "permissionless blockchain"

#### **EPOCH 2: Web3 Ascension (2021–2198)**
- **Setting:** Digital migration from streets to blockchain
- **Key Events:**
  - GraffPUNKS birth as decentralized Web3 movement
  - NFT launches, Sacred Chain protocols established
  - Concept of "unbuffable art" (erasure-resistant culture)
- **Philosophy:** Digital permanence as survival strategy

#### **EPOCH 3: The Great Collapse (2198 - Triple Fork Event)**
- **Setting:** Global blockchain fragmentation
- **Key Events:**
  - **TRIPLE FORK EVENT:** World Chain splits into 3 conflicting "realities"
  - Consensus evaporates; society fractures
  - "The Great Unravelling" / "The Chainfire" era begins
  - Rise of Chain-cults, barter kingdoms, digital dead-zones
  - Rogue AIs and digital warlords emerge
- **Consequence:** Humanity splits into incompatible blockchain factions
- **Philosophy:** Which timeline is "truth"?

#### **EPOCH 4: Dark Ages & Strongholds (2198–3000)**
- **Setting:** Post-collapse city-states
- **Key Events:**
  - Queens, NYC becomes primary fortified stronghold
  - Forkborn resistance emerges (Echo Ink technology develops)
  - Block Topia (AI-governed sterile order) rises
  - Street Kingdoms (anarchic creative freedom) form
- **Philosophy:** Survival through faction identity

#### **EPOCH 5: The Age of Grid (Year 3008 - CURRENT)**
- **Setting:** Established faction cold war
- **Key Events:**
  - 40 factions in uneasy détente
  - Jodie Zoom 2000 active as Forkborn Seer
  - GraffPUNKS operate as Immortal Architects
  - Gasless Ghosts & NULL The Prophet threaten existence
  - Queen Sarah P-fly rules Block Topia with iron AI governance
- **Philosophy:** Reality is malleable; memory is power

#### **EPOCH 6: The Final Fork Prophecy (Year 3030 - FUTURE)**
- **Setting:** Apocalyptic system-wide collapse
- **Key Events:**
  - All realities threaten total erasure
  - Forkborn must choose which timeline persists
  - AETHER CHAIN may offer "Great Consensus" path
- **Philosophy:** "Only unbreakable chains remain"
